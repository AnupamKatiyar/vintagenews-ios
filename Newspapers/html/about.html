Welcome to Vintage News

This app allows you to browse through the entire collection of the Library of Congress' Chronicling America project. This app is entirely unaffiliated with the Library of Congress.

Open Source Attributions

In addition to the data provided by the Library of Congress, this app wouldn't be possible without the generous iOS developer community and their open source components.
